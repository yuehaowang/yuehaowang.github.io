I am a final year M.Phil. student at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, where I am advised by Prof. Qi Dou and fortunate to work with Prof. Tianfan Xue and Prof. Xiaoguang Han. Before that, I obtained my B.Eng. degree at ShanghaiTech University. Outside of academics, my hobbies include playing the (electric) guitar, creative coding, and designing games (check out my showcase).
Research

My topics of interest include computer graphics, 3D vision, computational photography, and their intersections with machine learning. In particular, I work on projects in neural rendering and 3D reconstruction.
