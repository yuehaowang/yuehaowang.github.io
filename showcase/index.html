Fever in Music

As an enthusiast of rock, metal, and blues, I spend my free time on honing my guitar skills and crafting original songs when I'm not immersed in research. My passion for music is deeply rooted in the iconic sound of vintage bands such as Led Zeppelin and The Beatles. I draw profound inspiration from legendary guitarists like Jimmy Page, Slash, Eric Clapton, and Stevie Ray Vaughan, to name just a few.

Spectrum As an amateur "songwriter", I made this toy music album with Apple's Garageband™ loop packs. Each composition in the album reflects my inner emotions. Drawing inspiration from the masterpieces of various artists, I crafted each track to embrace diverse music styles.
